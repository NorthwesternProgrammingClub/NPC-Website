Northwestern Programming Club

This is the future home page of the Northwestern Programming Club (NPC).

Our Mission

We are a group of aspiring programmers.

We hope to create a collaborative environment for students working on computer-related projects, homework, and assignments.

We strive to be a collective resource for those who do not have a lot of programming experience, but are interested in coding and want to get involved outside of the classroom.

About Us

Established in 2015, the Northwestern Programming Club (NPC) was created after the founders noticed that there was a lack of a club on campus that promoted and encouraged programming to people of all skill levels.
